Why is this important? Why email Baleka Mbete
In south Africa, members of parliment usually vote along party lines, even if they have a different opinion. If the vote is made secret, this will allow MPs to vote based on their opinion, and not what their political party dictates. As Speaker of the National Assembly, Baleka Mbete has the power to declare the vote secret.
